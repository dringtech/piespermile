Welcome to
Pies per Mile

Pies per Mile is here to help you become more healthy by increasing the amount of exercise you take and making healthier food choices. It has a number of handy tools that will:

  • Help you choose a mode of transport that integrates appropriate exercise into your daily routine.
  • Suggest recreational routes in your local area that you can explore.
  • Provide healthy and nutritious meal suggestions.

One of the most successful ways to improve health is calorie counting, as keeping food intake in balance with energy expended is the fundamental goal of a healthy lifestyle. Let's face it though, we all find it difficult to picture a calorie. It can feel like a Wednesday afternoon maths lesson trying to keep on top of it all.

At the heart of the app is the standardised pie of 314 calories. All the choices in the app are presented with a pie value, helping you make the choice that's right for your lifestyle.

Over time, Pies per Mile learns about your preferences and will make suggestions based on your level of fitness.
